# Milestone Tracker

Check current progress against your milestone plan, identify the critical path, and flag slippage against the tolerance in your practice profile — distinguishing genuine timeline threats from cosmetic schedule noise. For anything at risk or slipped, states the knock-on effect on downstream milestones or the overall end date.

**Command:** `/pmo:milestone-tracker`

Provide the milestone plan and current actuals or forecasts, then invoke this agent or run the command directly.
