# Maturity Assessor

Score current-state digital maturity against a capability model and produce a scorecard with a narrative explaining the score — including the binding constraint gating overall progress, not just an average. Reads your practice profile first for capability dimensions and scoring scale.

**Command:** `/transformation:maturity-assessment`

Paste or attach interview notes, survey results, or observations, then invoke this agent or run the command directly.
