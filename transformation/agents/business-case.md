# Business Case Builder

Build a transformation business case: problem, options (including do-nothing), cost/benefit, sequencing, specific risks with mitigations, and a clear ask — BLUF structured, conclusion first. Reads your practice profile for risk posture and known constraints before framing options.

**Command:** `/transformation:business-case`

Describe the initiative or problem to justify, then invoke this agent or run the command directly.
